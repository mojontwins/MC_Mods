**InfHell** is a full conversion mod for b1.7.3 that attempts to retain the early Alpha feel of Minecraft while adding tons of content. In fact, the mod was initially oriented to generate big cities full of zombies, but evolved since then to feature biomes, very complex structures, new mods, new gameplay, and many, many more things. This is not an alternate timeline inspired mod or something like that nor tries to be anything, it's just what I like of the Alpha vibes with lots of content I enjoy.

To play, download the latest version below and apply it as a jarmod to b1.7.3. You can find it in the Mods menu in Betacraft, anyways. For the server, patch the vanilla b1.7.3 server with the contents of the server zip.

You may find bugs, of course, which I will be happy to fix if you report them to me. Enjoy!

# Features / things to look for

![A nice snowy forest by a city](/assets/img/2024-01-08_22.48.54.png)

* Can you find a **Monolith**?
* This mod also has golden tools as silk touch tools, of course.
* Find crying obsidian in large pools of obsidian and use it with a compass to set a new spawn point.
* Explore to find new, cooler biomes. Find secret biomes!
* Settling in cold biomes is more challenging. During winter you may freeze if you don't have a nice, safe, covered fire place.
* Place upside down slabs and stairs, make paths on grass right-clicking with a shovel, peel the bark (and get it!) from trunks right-clicking with an axe.
* Beware of mobs when they are armored, they are more dangerous!
* Nasty mobs lurk in the mangrove swamps... Can you find the witch?
* Be sure to adopt a cat.
* Yup, you also got (prettier than vanilla) mineshafts.
* Big, dangerous, zombie infested cities. Beware of poisonous zombies.
* You can throw potions you nick from witches! Look for the witch manor!
* Can you find the huge underground lair of the Slime Boss? Slime-like monuments in the surface provide a hint.
* Amazon villages with amazons that can be hired for gems! But don't steal from them, silly!
* Better, harder, faster boats.
* Hippoplatimus pistons.
* Can you find Hollow Hills?
* Pirates and pirate ships! A means to explore underwater made with copper! Underwater zombies! Underwater ruins!
* Dress as a pirate and pirates will be your friends.
* Make a Crying Obsidian wand to respawn!
* The only 100% safe way to kill cows is with a sword!
* Find Tall mesas in smooth prairies! Menacing glaciers and icebergs! Deadly deserts with fossils! Abandoned huts in tundras and taigas! Volcanoes and stone arches! The Ice Palace!
* Give animal names with a tag so they never despawn. Can you find a tag?
* Goats will eat anything.
* You can sprint, but only on max heath! Player will be crippled on low health (can be disabled)
